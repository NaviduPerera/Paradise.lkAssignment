Welcom to Sri Lanka




Hiking

A walk around Ella is a must-do activity for anybody visiting Sri Lanka. Ella is a little town in the country's gorgeous highlands with remarkable natural beauty. Only a train station, a main road, and a few guesthouses make up the city. The vistas are beautiful, and the forest is thick. Waterfalls, tea plantations, and a variety of other tourist attractions may be found in Ella. Ella Rock is a breathtaking place with a difficult hiking trail winding through flora and stones. The peak of the rocky outcropping provides panoramic views of the surrounding area.

Whale watching mirissa

Mirissa, with its abundance of dolphins, would be an ideal place in Sri Lanka for whale viewing. Southern Coast Whales sightseeing crew Mirissa Whale Warriors Thanks to the crew, live blowholes and giant aquatic animal monsters may be seen. It is Sri Lanka's most dependable Whale Watching Company, offering you the most beautiful and surprisingly live views of whales. Mirissa whale warriors can be spotted in the water with blue whales, Bryde's whales, Sperm whales, Fin whales, Killer whales, Common dolphins, and turtles.
